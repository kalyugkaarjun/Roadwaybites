Sattvik (vegetarian)

Sattvik restaurant is a popular vegetarian restaurant located in the Select City Walk Mall in Saket, South Delhi. It offers a wide range of vegetarian dishes from Indian, Chinese, and Continental cuisines, and is known for its healthy and nutritious food options.

The restaurant follows the Sattvik philosophy of cooking, which means that the food is prepared without the use of onion, garlic, and other pungent spices that are believed to stimulate the senses and increase aggression. Instead, the restaurant uses herbs and spices that are believed to have a calming effect on the mind and body.

Some of the most popular dishes on the menu include the Paneer Tikka, Palak Patta Chaat, and the Dal Makhani. The restaurant also offers a variety of desserts and beverages, including their signature Sattvik Cooler made with fresh fruits and herbs.

The ambiance of the restaurant is warm and inviting, with traditional Indian decor and comfortable seating arrangements. The restaurant also features a private dining area for groups and events.

Overall, Sattvik restaurant in Select City Walk is a great option for those looking for healthy and delicious vegetarian food in South Delhi.
